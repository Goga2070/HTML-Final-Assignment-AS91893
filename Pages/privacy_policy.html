The Maunga Club's Privacy Policy

The Maunga Club is aware of and respects your concern for your privacy as well as the confidentiality and security of any information we may collect about you as a customer or website visitor. Your privacy is important to the Maunga Club. As you engage with The Maunga Club, your personal information will be handled in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

The Maunga Club will continue to work on giving you better, more personalized services as well as more effective websites as the hotel's website grows and technology progresses. The Maunga Club advises you to frequently examine our privacy statement because changes may be made.

The Maunga Club's overall approach to protecting your privacy is described in this privacy statement.

Your Personal Information Is Important to Us

Information that identifies you or might identify you is referred to as "personal information". Your name, address, contact phone number, email address, date of birth, passport and visa information, transaction information, and financial details are some examples of the personal information we may gather. Additionally, we could retain a record of the goods and services you purchase from us.

If you provide an email address or mobile number as part of the personal information provided, The Maunga Club will only contact you by email or SMS if you consent to this method of communication.

The Maunga Club is dedicated to safeguarding your personal information and abiding by the Information Privacy Principles (IPPs) in the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 (NZPA). Our privacy policy, in our opinion, adheres to industry best practises and is also fair. You may occasionally be able to communicate with us in an anonymous or fictitious manner, but there are some circumstances in which we may not be able to give you the information or services you require if you withhold certain information from us.

The Information We Collect About You

The Maunga Club only gathers personal data that is reassuringly required for or directly associated with one or more of its operations or activities. For instance, The Maunga Club gathers your personal data in order to supply and bill you for the hotel accommodations and other goods and services you request. In order to better understand your needs and preferences, enhance our services, and present offers that may be useful to you, we may also gather personal information about you. For instance, when you complete a contact form, we gather the information you provide so we may give you information. In order to better serve you, we preserve the information you provide when asked what you would like to see.

There may be circumstances in which the private data you give us or that we gather qualifies as "sensitive information" under the APA. According to the APA, "sensitive information" refers to personal data that can be used to determine or infer a person's racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, or philosophical beliefs, as well as their membership in a professional association or trade union, their sexual preferences or practises, their criminal history, their health, and their biometric and genetic information. Only in compliance with and to the extent allowed by the APPs will we gather and use sensitive information about you.

The Maunga Club's Use of “Cookies”

"Cookies" are used by The Maunga Club to improve your experience on the websites. Cookies are little data files that websites transmit to the hard drive of your computer to save information. Most Web browsers are configured by default to allow cookies. You may, however, configure your browser to reject cookies if you do not want to receive any. If you disable cookies, we won't be able to, for instance, fill up forms with information you've already given us automatically.

Cookies by themselves don't include any personal data about you. They only serve as a special code that informs us of what our visitors find intriguing and helpful on our websites, sort of like an ongoing survey. Cookies may identify the user's browser, however the Maunga Club does not store any personally identifiable information in them.

Accessing, Changing & Deleting Information We Keep About You

We value your confidence in The Maunga Club much. Please email us at support@maungaclub.com if you ever wish to know exactly what personal information we currently have on file about you or to amend any information. We will answer to your email within a fair amount of time. Unless we believe there is a legitimate cause to withhold the information under the APA, NZPA, or other applicable legislation, we will grant you access to the information. If we decline to let you access or update the personal data we have on you, we will explain why in that denial. Additionally, you can ask that the personal data The Maunga Club has on file for you be updated by sending an email to support@maungaclub.com. If you submit a statement on the proposed adjustments, we will attach this statement to our records in the event that The Maunga Club and you dispute on whether the information is accurate, full, and up-to-date.

Complaints

Please get in touch with us in the first instance if you have a problem about how The Maunga Club is handling your personal information. You have the option to escalate your issue to the New Zealand Privacy Commissioner if you're not happy with the response you get. Please visit the New Zealand Privacy Commissioner's website at http://privacy.org.nz if you would like further information regarding privacy-related problems in New Zealand.

You understand and agree that by using the The Maunga Club website, services, or visiting our location, you are agreeing to be bound by the terms and conditions of The Maunga Club as well as this privacy statement. This is The Maunga Club's most recent privacy statement. It takes the place of any previous privacy statement for the Hotel Britomart website that has been posted there. The Maunga Club may update this privacy statement at any moment by posting the updated terms on this website.

How to contact us

Contact The Maunga Club's privacy representative at support@maungaclub.com if you want to access or update your personal information, have a complaint about how The Maunga Club handled your personal information, or have questions about this privacy statement.
